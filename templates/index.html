Services

We provide variety of industrial services

  • Computer Aided Design (CAD)

    We can create CAD drawing for new and existing equipments using Solidworks, Catia, AutoCAD or Inventor.

    These CAD model can be used for any further analysis such as FEA and mode shape analysis. Futhermore, we also provide consulations and analysis on your designs.

    Sample CAD design works done by the reseach unit.

  • Condition Based Maintaince (CBM)

    The goal of condition-based maintenance is to uncover equipment failure before it happens, so maintenance can be done exactly when needed. Thus saving both money and time.

    We provide CBM services for any industrial equipment and has a vast array of sensors such as acclerometers, flow sensors, strain gauges and microphones to monitor equipment health.

  • Finite Element (FE) and Fatigue Analysis

    FEA is a computerized numerical method which divides the problem into discrete number of elements and approximates the solution of the total system by solving algebraic equations.
    This method is greatly useful for identify whether part of the system would fail due to stress and make necessary changes.

    Fatigue analysis is a FEA where results provide a prediction of fatigue life of a product. So fatigue analysis is highly important on as it is the most common type of mechanical failure in systems that are subjected to cyclic loadings.

    Few sample FEA and Fatigue analysis are given below

  • Topology Optimization

    Topology Optimization is a mathematical method that optimizes material layout within a given design space, for a given set of loads, boundary conditions and constraints with the goal of maximizing the performance of the system.  

    Using to Topology Optimization material and energy useage of the equipments can be drastically reduced while improving its strength and durability.

  • Measurment and Model Validation

    We are capable in data acquiring at high accuracy and speed using variaty of sensors including acclerometers, pressure sensors, encorders, strain gauges, microphones etc.

    Futhermore, we provide modal analysis for any structure and also provide specialize measuremt for the automotive industry such as suspension and emission testing.

  • Kinematic and Dynamic Analysis

    Kinematic and dynamic analysis is instrumental when designing vehicles and to tune all subsystems.

    Using MSC Adams Car, we can preform various type of simulations such as, straight-line acceleration & deceleration, lane change, constant radius cornering, and cornering on a inclined plane.
